Resource Profile:

Official URL: http://koppeltaal.nl/fhir/StructureDefinition/KT2Device Version: 0.8
Draft as of 2023-02-07 Responsible: VZVZ Computable Name: KT2_Device

Copyright/Legal: VZVZ

The (FHIR) Device (resource) is a representation of an application that is used in the provision of healthcare without being substantially changed through that activity. The device may be a module, portal or eHealth app.

Usage:

  • Refer to this Resource Profile: KT2_AuditEvent and KT2_ResourceOrigin
